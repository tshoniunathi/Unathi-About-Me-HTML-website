My journey


Why coding?

We live in a world that is driven by technology, the art of coding has evolved from being basic text lines into a universal language of empowerment and innovation and my curiosity knows no boundaries.
I am on a journey to learn an art that can open doors of problem solving, creative development and the understanding of nuances in a digital world.
As a woman in science, I have always been intrigued by the impact our environments have on us, this is an extension of that.
Fundamentally, coding is a manifestation of human imagination; a way that enables us to create solutions with the potential to transform lives by communicating our ideas to machines.
When learn how to programme, we step into a world where concepts take shape and that is a power that can bridge the gap between our ideas and reality.
